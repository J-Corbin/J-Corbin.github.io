Here are some of my favorite quotes that I think you might like too. :)

"To live is the rarest thing in the world. Most people exist, that is all."

- Oscar Wilde

"I am patient with stupidity but not with those who are proud of it."

- Edith Sitwell

"To err is human, to forgive divine."

- Alexander Pope

"Two things are infinite, the universe and human stupidity, and I'm not sure about the universe."

- Albert Einstein

"You know you're in love when you can't fall asleep because reality is finally better than your dreams."

- Dr. Seuss

"In three words I can sum up everything I've learned about life: it goes on."

- Robert Frost

"It is better to remain silent at the risk of being thought a fool than to talk and remove all doubt of it."

- Maurice Switzer

"It does not do to dwell on dreams and forget to live."

- J.K. Rowling

"To find yourself, think for yourself."

- Socrates

"I will not let anyone walk through my mind with their dirty feet."

- Mahatma Gandhi

This next one is one of my all time favorite quotes from my favorite book, The Name of the Wind.

“In many ways, unwise love is the truest love. Anyone can love a thing because. That’s as easy as putting a penny in your pocket. But to love something despite. To know the flaws and love them too. That is rare and pure and perfect.”

- Patrick Rothfuss
